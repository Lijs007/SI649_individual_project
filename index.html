Possion Distribution:

The Poisson distribution is a discrete probability distribution that expresses the probability of a given number of events
occurring in a fixed interval of time or space if these events occur with a known constant rate and independently of the time since the last event.

And this is the bar chart of number of incidents occured every week of the chosen year:

We cannot decide whether it follows a Possion Distribution through this graph.

Actually, we prefre to use frequency plot to anaylize this distribution. Here's the plot of the number of weeks with specific incidence occured:

It seems that it follows the Possion Distribution basicly. However, it's easy to see that the amout of data is not large enough.

So let's try the data of all years(2014 ~ 2018 OCT). And the ideally expected result is plotted in blue:

We can see the two graphs are very similar.
